PLAY

GDD

TS is a game of the action genre with a top-down view.
In it, the player faces another inside a defined enclosure. Each of them controls a tank that moves across the stage to try to kill the enemy. Before each game, players choose the weapons (each with different properties) for their respective tanks, which will not change during the same.

Players control their tank with WASD for movement and using the mouse to aim and attack. Each tank has two categories of weapons: primary and secondary. In each of these categories the player has 2 options of which he can only take one to battle.
